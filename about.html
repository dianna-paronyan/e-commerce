About us

Creative Silver is a specialized salon of silver jewelry which are born as a result of original approach. We represent silver in combination with semi-precious raw natural stones, enamel, leather, wood, brass. The brand was founded in 2022. Each piece of jewelry here is handcrafted. Such products cannot be called just jewelry - they are real works of art that require love towards the profession, talent and hard work.
